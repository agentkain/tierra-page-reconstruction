Roundup Linked To Multiple Cancers

Roundup, the popular weed killer introduced by Monsanto in the 1970s, uses glyphosate as its main ingredient. Scientific studies have linked glyphosate exposure to various types of cancer, particularly Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma.

Roundup Lawsuit Updates

About 170,000 lawsuits have been filed against Bayer and Monsanto due to harm caused by Roundup.1

Bayer has paid nearly $11 billion in settlements so far.2

Bayer lost 6 trials in late 2023 and early 2024 resulting in a total of over $4 billion in verdicts.3

The World Health Organization (WHO) classified glyphosate as "probably carcinogenic to humans" in 2015.4
